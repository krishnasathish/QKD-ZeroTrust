🧠 Zero-Trust Key Service (ZK-Service)

The operational core of the architecture. Unlike traditional QKD which just pushes keys, the ZK-Service enforces continuous identity verification and device posture assessment.

It acts as a middleware that leases short-lived, context-bound keys to applications only after validating policy compliance (e.g., "Is the receiving node in a trusted geo-location?").
